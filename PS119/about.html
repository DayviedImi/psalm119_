Discover the Core Values of Our Challenge

At the heart of the Psalm 119 National Values Challenge lies a commitment to empowering students through the memorization of scripture. Our mission is to foster integrity, perseverance, and community engagement among participants. We believe that by memorizing Psalm 119, students not only enhance their knowledge but also develop essential life skills that contribute to their personal growth. Our vision is to create a supportive environment where students can thrive, connect with their peers, and engage meaningfully with their communities. The challenge is not just about memorization; it's about building character and leadership skills that will last a lifetime. Join us in this transformative journey!
