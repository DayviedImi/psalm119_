Our Mission and Values

The Psalm 119 National Values Challenge aims to empower students through scripture memorization, fostering integrity, perseverance, community engagement, and personal growth. Join us in this transformative journey.

Our Mission

We empower students through the memorization of Psalm 119, fostering character development, leadership skills, community engagement and Nation Building. Together, we can make a difference in their lives.

Our History

Established in 2018, the Psalm 119 National Values Challenge has worked to build a culture of scripture memorization, fostering community dedicated to personal growth and Nation Building.

Mastering Knowledge Application in the Challenge

The Knowledge Application stage is a pivotal part of the Psalm 119 National Values Challenge. Participants engage in a CBT test that evaluates their understanding and application of the memorized verses, fostering critical thinking and deeper comprehension.

  • Interactive CBT test for comprehensive evaluation
  • Enhances critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Prepares students for real-world applications
